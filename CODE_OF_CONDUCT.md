# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Pragma is a small open-source project. Anyone who participates — by opening issues, submitting pull requests, or joining discussions — is expected to be respectful and constructive.

Specifically:
- Be direct and honest, not dismissive or hostile
- Criticise ideas, not people
- Accept that maintainers make final decisions on scope and direction

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Personal attacks, insults, or harassment of any kind
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Sustained disruptive behaviour in issues or pull requests

## Enforcement

Reports of unacceptable behaviour can be sent to **pragma@lightweb.it**. The maintainer will review and respond within 7 days. Responses may include closing the issue/PR, removing comments, or blocking the account.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces: the GitHub repository, issues, pull requests, and any other official communication channel.
